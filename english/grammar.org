*** 语法笔记
**** 基本语法
#+begin_center
主语 + 谓语动词 + 宾语
#+end_center

**** 谓语动词
1. 不及物动词
2. 单及物动词
3. 双及物动词
4. 复杂及物动词
5. (连)系动词

**** 非谓语动词(/非限定动词/)
- 动词不定式
#+begin_example
To be or not to be, that is the question
#+end_example
- 动名词
- 现在分词
#+begin_example
现在分词可用作形容词
The story is interesting
这里的interest转现在分词interesting用作形容词有趣的
现在分词作状语
Hearing the news, ...
#+end_example
- 过去分词
#+begin_example
The rabbit will eat minced carrot next week
minced为过去分词做定语表搅碎的
#+end_example
**** 动词时态
| 时间     | - | 状态     |
| 现在     | - | 一般     |
| 过去     | - | 完成     |
| 将来     | - | 进行     |
| 过去将来 | - | 完成进行 |
- 一般现在
  #+begin_example
  I eat a carrot
  #+end_example
- 一般过去(/动词的过去式/)
  #+begin_example
  I ate a carrot
  #+end_example
- 一般将来(/will+原形/)
  #+begin_example
  I will eat a carrot
  #+end_example
- 一般过去将来
  #+begin_example
  I said i would eat a carrot
  #+end_example
- 现在完成(/助动词have的变位+动词的过去分词/)
  #+begin_example
  I have eaten a carrot
  #+end_example
- 过去完成(/have的过去变位+动词的过去分词/)
  #+begin_example
  I had eaten a carrot
  #+end_example
- 将来完成
  #+begin_example
  I will have eaten a carrot
  #+end_example
- 过去将来完成
  #+begin_example
  I said i would have eaten a carrot by 1PM tomorrow
  #+end_example
- 现在进行(/助动词be的变位+动词的现在分词/)
  #+begin_example
  I am eating a carrot
  #+end_example
- 过去进行(/be的过去变位+动词的现在分词/)
  #+begin_example
  I was eating a carrot
  #+end_example
- 将来进行
  #+begin_example
  I will be eating a carrot for lunch tommorrow
  #+end_example
- 过去将来进行
  #+begin_example
  I said i would be eating a carrot at 1PM tomorrow
  #+end_example
- 现在完成进行(/have的变位+been+动词的现在分词/)
  #+begin_example
  I have been eating a carrot
  #+end_example
- 过去完成进行(/have的过去变位+been+动词的现在分词/)
  #+begin_example
  I had been eating a carrot
  #+end_example
- 将来完成进行
  #+begin_example
  I will have been eating a carrot
  #+end_example
- 过去将来完成进行
  #+begin_example
  I said i would have been eating a carrot for 2 hours by 1PM tomorrow
  #+end_example
